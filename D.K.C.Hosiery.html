Our Products

Pullovers

A pullover is a versatile garment. It is typically made of knit fabric and is designed to be worn loosely. The Classic Crew neck pullover is a popular choice, featuring a round neckline and a relaxed fit.V-neck pullovers have a V-shaped neckline that adds a touch of elegance and can be layered over collared shirts for a more formal look.Turtleneck pullovers have a high, close-fitting neckline that provides extra warmth and a stylish, sleek appearance.Hooded pullovers, commonly known as hoodies, feature a hood attached to the neckline, offering both comfort and a casual aesthetic. These are just a few examples of the many types of pullovers made in our hosiery.

Windcheaters

A windcheater, also known as a windbreaker, is a lightweight outerwear garment designed to protect the wearer from wind and light rain. It is typically made of nylon or polyester fabric that is resistant to wind and water.The Classic Zip-up windcheater features a front zipper closure, elastic cuffs, and a drawstring waist or hem to provide a snug fit and prevent wind from entering. Softshell windcheaters are made of a stretchy and breathable fabric, often with a fleece lining for added warmth, while still providing windproof and water-resistant properties.

Modi-Jackets

Modi jackets are a type of men's outerwear inspired by the traditional Indian achkan or sherwani. They are commonly made from luxurious fabrics such as silk, brocade, or jacquard, and often feature intricate embroidery or embellishments. These jackets are versatile and can be worn with traditional Indian attire like kurta-pajama or paired with Western outfits for a fusion look. They are available in various types, including single-breasted and double-breasted designs, with options for sleeveless or full sleeves. Modi jackets add a touch of elegance and sophistication to any ensemble, making them a popular choice for formal occasions, weddings, or cultural events.
